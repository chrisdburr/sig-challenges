AI-Enabled Digital Twin Challenges

Below are individual challenges related to the assurance of AI-enabled digital twin technologies across various sectors. Each challenge has different components which can be filtered by sector, capability, and other attributes. The challenges reflect key requirements to ensure the reliability and trustworthiness of artificial intelligence in digital twin applications.


  • Significance: The potential impact of addressing this challenge on digital twin ecosystem, including benefits to safety, performance, adoption, and broader societal value.
  • Complexity: The combined technical, organisational, and regulatory difficulty of addressing the challenge.
  • Readiness: The current level of community preparedness to address the challenge, including available expertise, methods, tools, consensus on approaches, and access to necessary resources.

This webpage has been adapted from the AI Carbon Challenge Cards produced by the AI for Decarbonisation's Virtual Centre for Excellence (ADViCE).
